Since 1955, families from all over BC and Yukon have stayed at Easter Seals House while their child or loved one receives care and treatment at nearby medical facilities. Open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, Easter Seals House is located just blocks away from Vancouver’s best hospitals and treatment centres.

With 49 self-contained double occupancy rooms, in-suite kitchenettes, two recreational lounges, children’s play room, two family sized kitchens, onsite laundry, WIFI, outdoor garden, 24/7 front desk service, Snack Shoppe, underground parking, and housekeeping services, Easter Seals House offers comfortable amenities for families in need.

Staying at the House

No matter how long they plan to stay, families experience a warm welcome. It’s the perfect home away from home. We’re located at 3981 Oak Street, Vancouver. Just look for the 7 foot ‘Spirit Artwork’ statues lining the front sidewalk.
